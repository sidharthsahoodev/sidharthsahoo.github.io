// about

The short version

"You think it, I'll build it." That is what I told my co-founder on day one, and it still sums me up. I am resourceful, and I ship.

For the past two years I have been the core engineer behind PACCA AI at Emids, an agentic-AI platform that speeds up the whole software delivery lifecycle. I designed its multi-agent orchestration layer and shipped a suite of accelerators that cut engineering effort by more than 60 percent.

Before that I co-founded a startup as CTO, led delivery teams, and built Android fintech products used at real scale. I move comfortably between deep engineering, product thinking, and the people side that actually makes things ship.

What I care about is the unglamorous part: evaluation, guardrails, governance. The gap between a slick AI demo and a system people trust is almost never the model. It is everything around it. That is the work I want to do.
